Customer Base Audit — KazMarket
Project: Customer Base Audit
Course: Quantitative and Digital Skills Portfolio
Framework: Ascarza, Hardie, Ross & Fader — Conducting a Customer-Base Audit (HBR)
Dataset: KazMarket online retailer — 7M+ transactions, 2021–2024
Project Overview
This project applies the customer-base audit methodology to KazMarket, an online retailer.
The goal is to answer five managerial questions using three analytical tools:
1. Who are our best and worst customers? → Decile Analysis (2024)
2. How does customer behavior change over time? → Single-Cohort Analysis (Q1 2021)
3. How do different cohorts compare? → Three-Cohort Comparison (Q3 2021 / Q4 2021
/ Q4 2022)
4. How healthy is the customer base? → Annual Performance Summary (2021–2024)
5. What does this mean for growth? → Executive synthesis and recommendations
